How it works

Not advice. Evidence.

Step 01
🚶

Someone takes the path

A person makes a major career choice — joins a startup, pursues a master's, switches industries, takes a gap year. They live it and see where it leads.

Step 02
📋

They file a cost report

Using HINDSIGHT's structured format, they document exactly what the choice cost — money, time, emotional toll, missed opportunities — and the real outcome vs. expectations.

Step 03
🎯

You get matched

When you're at a decision point, HINDSIGHT surfaces stories from people who started exactly where you are. Same field, same city, same trade-offs. Specific, not generic.
